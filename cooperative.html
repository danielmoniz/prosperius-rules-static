Cooperative Play

Cooperative Play

Although Prosperius can sometimes feel cooperative, you are still competing against your opponents in the regular mode of play. Cooperative Mode changes that.

Select a scenario from the options below. Each scenario has a corresponding summary card(s) that can be used for easy reference.

Population Influx

Populations are booming. Your villages need to grow enough to attract larger populations before neighbouring towns render you irrelevant.

Have each village meet the Tier requirement by the given rounds.

Tier # Rounds (Easy) # Rounds (Medium) # Rounds (Hard)
2 6 5 4
3 9 9 7
4 11 11 9
✓ 13 13 11
Failure

Each village that fails to be at the required Tier by the given round immediately loses 5 Prosperity. This will make it that much harder to meet the next Tier requirement.

The Great Cathedral (idea)

  1. Players work together to finish building The Great Cathedral in X rounds.
  2. Requires:
    • loads of basic resources.
    • multiple stages of building (tracked on a card/sheet), some of which require education.
    • Luxury goods.
    • manual labour (taking actions on the cathedral itself)
    • Get bonuses (eg. Prosperity) for completing the Cathedral on time.
      • This should help to keep the game moving, rather than only slow it down!

Black Plague (idea)

  1. Track sickness by heart tokens.
    • People get sick (use Heart tokens, ideally only one per player).
      • As soon as someone hits Tier 2, one village each round has one of their population become sick.
      • Any village already with a sick population gets one more of their population sick.
      • If all of their population are sick, that player loses 5 Prosperity and that disease spreads to their left instead.
    • Sickness can spread from village to village through trade.
    • Players must visit the Town Physician for health supplies to keep the disease from spreading and/or heal their villagers.
    • Uses separate Town Event deck with Disease Spreads cards.
  2. Track health with heart tokens and have bad things happen depending on number of unhealthy population in villages.

Baby Boom (idea)

Your villagers have come back from the war and population is rising. Ideas:

  1. Players gain all 5 population after the any player hits Tier 2.
    • Players must then all finish the game within some number of rounds.
