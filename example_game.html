Example Gameplay

Example Game (first few rounds)

Round 1

World Phase
  1. Flip season to winter (if it isn't already because it's round 1).
  2. Flip the Town Event deck to reveal a No News is Good News (nothing happens).
Passive Phase
  • Nothing to do yet.
Act
  1. Take a Village Centre action to build a Farm, then wait for your next action.
  2. Gather a Vegetable to ensure your Bread stores lasts longer.
    • Line up your available food right away!
  3. Take back pieces when everyone is done.
Trade
  • There is probably nothing to trade at this point, unless certain locations were built and specific resources were gathered.
Prosper
  • Eat your food (1 Bread, 1 Vegetable) and gain 2 Prosperity because the Vegetable elevates the Bread to being quality food!

Round 3

World Phase
  1. Pick a new first player if the previous first player is tied or no longer in last (passes to the left to the player with the fewest points).
  2. Flip Season to Summer. No need to grow saplings/trees/wheat or replenish animals.
Passive Phase
  • Nothing to do yet.
Act
  1. You build the Hunting Cabin (someone else has already taken the Bakery!).
  2. Use your Farm while it's still summer.
