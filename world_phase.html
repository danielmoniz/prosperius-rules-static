World Phase

World Phase

In Prosperius, the world evolves around you. The World Phase is as follows:

  1. If a new Tier has been reached:
    • Reveal a new Town card. Discard the previous Town card.
    • Reveal new locations for the new Tier by adding them to the central supply.
  2. Determine the new first player to be the player with the lowest score.
    • If there is a tie, the previous first player passes the token to the first player on their left that is tied with them.
  3. Flip the Season card and immediately perform its effects.
