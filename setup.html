Setup

Setup

Set up your village

For each player:

  1. Pick one of each of the two player board halves. Decide as a group whether or not to do them randomly. Place both halves together in front of you so that the border is on the outside.
  2. Take one of each of the following cards: Village Centre, Gather, Stockpile, and Home.
    • Place your Village Centre on the big X on your Player Board.
    • Place Home partially underneath your player board so that only the top two green rows are showing.
    • Place the other two cards near your Player Board.
  3. Take 1 Wood and 2 Bread pieces from the supply. Place them in your Stockpile.
  4. Take two population tokens of your preferred colour from the supply.
  5. Place the corresponding coloured marker on the Prosperity (score) board at 0 points.
  6. Ensure the game’s resource/good pieces are accessible to everybody; they will be using them regularly.

Set up the cards

  1. Sort the game’s locations by removing any cards that require more players than are playing. Some cards specify player numbers on the back at the bottom. Then group the remaining cards by colour (ie. tier).
  2. Lay out all cards from the first tier (indicated by a green stripe) in such a way that they are accessible and easy to read for all players. Place the remaining cards in a pile to the side.
  3. Put the Season card in the middle of the playing area with the Summer side face up. The first turn will be summer.
  4. Reveal the Food Surplus Town card. Place it beside the season card. Shuffle the remaining Town cards and place them underneath or nearby.
  5. Put the Town Market card in the middle of the playing area so that everyone can reach it.

Pick the first player

  1. The player who had the most prosperous weekend goes first. Place the Straggler Token in front of them.
