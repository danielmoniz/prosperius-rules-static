Act

Act

Phase order

The Act phase is where most of your time will be spent.

  1. Starting with the first player, each player takes a single action (see Taking an Action below) using one of their unused workers.
    • Continue clockwise until all players have used their population to take actions.
  2. Once everyone has completed all possible actions, return your villagers home by placing them on the circles on your Home card.
  3. Discard degrading items.
    • Eg. if your population was wearing clothing, discard one clothing (it has been tattered over the course of the season).

Taking an action

  1. Place one population on a card that allows for an action to be taken.
    • This means that:
      1. All conditions are met (eg. it is currently the correct season, and so on).
      2. All costs can be paid. Your card should have placed on its demand icons the goods that will be spent for the action.
  2. Tell the next player you are done your turn (as you are just collecting pieces).
  3. Immediately perform the action.
    • Pay the cost.
    • Do what the action says, including all modifiers.
      • Eg. if the card says "+3 Grain", take 3 Grain from the bank.
    • Then, fulfill your demand!
      1. Set aside any goods that are demanded by your villagers. This includes food up to your current population. See the relevant reference card for a complete list.
      2. Place any gained goods onto any demand indicated on your cards.
      3. Place any remaining goods on your stockpile. These can be traded.
