Goods (oodles!)

Most of the pieces in Prosperius are goods, sometimes called resources. These are gathered, produced, and traded.

[add image]

Some goods are designed to be carried by your villagers. These goods can be placed directly onto Villager Tokens to be carried around. For example, clothes indicate that your population works effectively in the cold.

Education is not a "Good", but it is also carried by your villagers to indicate that they are permanently educated.

[add image]

Animals

Animals are not considered goods. They cannot be demanded or traded. There are sheep, pigs, and cows.

Straggler Token (ie. first player)

The Straggler Token indicates which player goes first in a round.
