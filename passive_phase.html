Passive Phase

Passive Phase

The passive phase is short but important. Any automatic effects in your village happen at this time.

Perform any passive effect (for which you meet the conditions) on your cards. They should be highlighted to be as clear as possible.

Some examples:

  • Animals reproduce in sheep/pig/cow pastures if there are at least two of them.
  • Add fish to Fishing Nets cards.
  • Vegetable Field provides one or more Vegetables to your stockpile.
  • ...etc.
