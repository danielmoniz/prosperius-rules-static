Gameplay Overview

Game Round Overview

There are 5 phases each round. Most of the phases are quick. The Act Phase is where you will spend most of your time.

Round order

  1. World Phase
    • (new tier setup if necessary)
    • Pick first player
    • Flip/resolve season card
  2. Passive Phase
    • Perform passive actions
  3. Act
    • Take actions
    • Return workers home
  4. Trade
    • Take turns offering goods
  5. Prosper
    • Calculate points
    • Discard food eaten
    • Discard excess food
    • Discard one clothing
